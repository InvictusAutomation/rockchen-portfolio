01 — The Problem

Copilots Are Still Sidekicks

Today's AI assistants live in browser sidebars — waiting to be summoned. They require manual triggering, context switching, and constant prompting.

  • Users must explicitly ask for help every time
  • AI has no awareness of the user's current workflow state
  • Interaction is limited to chat — no ambient intelligence
  • The burden of initiation is always on the human
