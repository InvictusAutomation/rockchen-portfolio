01

The Autonomy Gap

Existing assistants never act unless explicitly commanded. They're powerful tools that refuse to take initiative.

Research shows users expect intelligent systems to demonstrate understanding through proactive behavior. The absence of this creates a "dumb tool" perception regardless of actual capability. Voice Helper introduces adjustable agency levels — from passive listener to proactive collaborator.
02

Interactivity Gap

Complex tasks require multi-turn interactions with significant context tracking overhead placed on the user.

When users attempt multi-step goals, current assistants lose context or require repetitive clarification. Voice Helper maintains a rich context model and chains actions autonomously — reducing 8-turn interactions to 2-turn outcomes.
