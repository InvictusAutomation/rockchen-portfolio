The Problem

Current AI Copilots live in sidebars,
waiting to be summoned.

Today's AI assistants are passive tools — they require manual triggers, live in isolated UI panels, and never truly understand your workflow context.

Users spend more energy managing the AI than benefiting from it. The interaction model is fundamentally broken.
AI should be like a silent companion — proactive when appropriate, invisible when not. AI 应该像一个沉默的伙伴——适时主动，无需时不打扰

Why Voice Helper? Why now?
Hover to explore →
The gap between AI capability and AI usability has never been wider. LLMs can reason, plan, and create — yet our interfaces force users through text boxes and chat panels. Voice Helper bridges this gap by making AI interaction as natural as talking to a colleague sitting next to you.
