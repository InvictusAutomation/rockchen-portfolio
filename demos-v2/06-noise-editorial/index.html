004 — Problem Analysis

Current voice assistants are reactive. We need them to be proactive.

Existing solutions wait for commands. They don't anticipate needs, learn preferences over time, or take meaningful action without explicit instruction. The gap between what users expect and what's delivered is massive.

Voice Helper bridges that gap through autonomous decision-making combined with natural, intuitive voice interaction.
